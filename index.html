PAYG

With the PAYG model, customers can choose a pricing plan which involves no commitment financially or in terms of contract length. This new evergreen model, does exactly what it says on the tin: customers sign up to a usage plan in which they receive the product offering without being tied in to a defined commitment term, and only pay New Relic for their Users as well as Data ingested on a monthly basis in arrears. It can be cancelled at any time..
